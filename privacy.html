Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Cafe Pizza House. Your privacy and security are of paramount importance to us. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you browse our digital menu and place orders.

1. Information We Collect

To process your online orders and deliver fresh pizza to your doorstep, we collect the following checkout information:

  • Full Name: To identify you for the delivery/takeaway handoff.
  • WhatsApp Mobile Number: To send order status updates and dispatch the order details template.
  • Delivery Address: Only collected for Delivery orders, used strictly to route the delivery driver.
  • Email Address (Optional): Used to send digital bills or receipts.

2. Geolocation and Consent

We do not track your real-time GPS location in the background. If the site requests your browser location, it is only to help auto-fill the delivery address field for your convenience. You may decline this prompt, and manually type your address instead.

3. Data Retention and Sharing

We do not sell, lease, or share your contact details or address with any third-party marketing companies. Your order details are logged on our secure database solely to allow the kitchen staff to prepare your food and track sales analytics.

4. Client-Side Protection

We store your checkout contact information in your browser's local storage (`localStorage`) solely to auto-fill the order fields the next time you visit. This data stays local on your phone and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser cache.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request the deletion of your historical order data, please contact the restaurant manager directly via WhatsApp.
